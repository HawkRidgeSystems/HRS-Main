SOLIDWORKS User Group Meetings

The SOLIDWORKS User Group Network (SWUGN) has one mission – bringing SOLIDWORKS users together to learn from each other. Get info on upcoming meetings and events for your local SWUG, and find out more about your local SOLIDWORKS community.

User groups are a valuable resource for all members of the SOLIDWORKS community. The ability to network with peers and partners helps your company become more productive.

  • Learn more about SOLIDWORKS and SOLIDWORKS related products
  • Better educate yourself on how SOLIDWORKS can be used
  • Network with your peers
  • Share experiences and technical presentations that can help provide you and your company with valuable, real-world techniques and methodologies
  • Give-aways, food, and refreshments usually provided for each meeting
