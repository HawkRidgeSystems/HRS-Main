What is xBom?

xBom was created from the need to leverage the already-powerful Bill of Materials capabilities inside SOLIDWORKS Enterprise PDM to build more complete Bills of Materials using other data sources, without incurring the time and expense implementing a full PLM system.   xBom provides the flexibility and ease-of-use you already enjoy from SOLIDWORKS Enterprise PDM and Microsoft Excel, with the ability to easily incorporate and reconcile multiple data sources, into a single complete Bill of Materials, viewable in as many different forms, as departments that touch it.
